# TechWidget Pro - Frequently Asked Questions

## Getting Started

### What is TechWidget Pro?
TechWidget Pro is a next-generation productivity tool designed to streamline your workflow. It combines task management, document collaboration, and team communication in one integrated platform.

### System Requirements
- Operating System: Windows 10/11, macOS 10.15+, or Linux (Ubuntu 20.04+)
- RAM: Minimum 4GB, recommended 8GB
- Storage: 500MB free space
- Internet: Broadband connection required

### How do I create an account?
1. Visit https://techwidget.example.com
2. Click "Sign Up" in the top right corner
3. Enter your email address and create a password
4. Verify your email through the link sent to your inbox
5. Complete your profile setup

## Account Management

### How do I reset my password?
If you've forgotten your password:
1. Go to the login page
2. Click "Forgot Password" below the login form
3. Enter your registered email address
4. Check your email for a password reset link (arrives within 5 minutes)
5. Click the link and create a new password
6. Your new password must be at least 8 characters with one uppercase letter and one number

### Can I change my email address?
Yes, you can change your email address:
1. Log into your account
2. Go to Settings → Account → Email
3. Enter your new email address
4. Verify the change through the confirmation email
5. Your new email will be active immediately after verification

### How do I delete my account?
To permanently delete your account:
1. Navigate to Settings → Account → Privacy
2. Scroll to "Delete Account" section
3. Click "Request Account Deletion"
4. Confirm by entering your password
5. Your account and all data will be permanently deleted within 7 business days
6. You'll receive a confirmation email when deletion is complete

## Advanced Features

### What are webhooks and how do I set them up?
Webhooks allow TechWidget Pro to send real-time notifications to your other applications:
1. Go to Settings → Integrations → Webhooks
2. Click "Add Webhook"
3. Enter your endpoint URL
4. Select events to trigger webhook
5. Test the webhook connection
6. Save configuration
Available on Pro and Team plans only.

### Does TechWidget Pro have an API?
Yes! Our REST API is available for Pro and Team subscribers:
- Documentation: https://api.techwidget.example.com/docs
- Authentication: API keys or OAuth 2.0
- Rate limits: 1000 requests/hour (Pro), 5000 requests/hour (Team)
- SDKs available for Python, JavaScript, and Ruby
